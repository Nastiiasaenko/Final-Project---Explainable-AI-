# Real-World Datasets for Disinformation & Bias Detection

This list compiles datasets suitable for various tasks, including disinformation detection, bias identification (left/right leaning), and general analysis of political and social discourse.

## Datasets

1. **Fake News Classification:** Labeled fake news articles (no publisher info). [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/saurabhshahane/fake-news-classification)
2. **Fake News Detection:** Real vs. fake news articles. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/emineyetm/fake-news-detection-datasets)
3. **News Category Dataset:** Variety of news articles with metadata (no fake/real labels). [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rmisra/news-category-dataset) 
4. **Fake News Classification:** Fake news articles (no metadata). [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/saurabhshahane/fake-news-classification)
5. **BBC News Summary:** BBC news articles and summaries. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/pariza/bbc-news-summary)
6. **Financial News Sentiment Analysis:**  Financial news with sentiment labels. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ankurzing/sentiment-analysis-for-financial-news)
7. **Fake News:** Fake news with authors, dates, and other potentially relevant information. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mrisdal/fake-news)
8. **TextDB3:** Fake news dataset. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hassanamin/textdb3)
9. **Source-Based News Classification:** Fake news articles with source information. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ruchi798/source-based-news-classification)
10. **Fake or Real News:** Labeled fake and real news articles. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jillanisofttech/fake-or-real-news)
11. **Political Advertisements from Facebook:** Dataset of political ads. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mrmorj/political-advertisements-from-facebook)
12. **Political Social Media Posts:** Political posts from various social media platforms. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/crowdflower/political-social-media-posts) 
13. **United States Political Tweets:** Twitter timelines of US politicians. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tunguz/united-states-political-tweets)
14. **Political Tweets:** Global political tweets. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kaushiksuresh147/political-tweets)
15. **Political Emails:** Dataset of political emails. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/noahfinberg/political-emails) 
16. **Liberals vs Conservatives on Reddit:** Reddit posts from liberal and conservative users. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/neelgajare/liberals-vs-conservatives-on-reddit-13000-posts)
17. **Reddit Vaccine Myths:** Reddit posts discussing vaccine myths. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/code/fahadmehfoooz/reddit-vaccine-myths) 
18. **Democrat vs Republican Tweets:** Tweets from Democrat and Republican users. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kapastor/democratvsrepublicantweets)
19. **US Political Speeches:** Speeches by US politicians. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/beridzeg45/usa-political-speeches)
20. **CNN News and Highlights:** CNN news articles and highlights. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gowrishankarp/newspaper-text-summarization-cnn-dailymail)
21. **Google War News:** News articles related to war.  [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/armitaraz/google-war-news)
22. **Reddit Conspiracy Theory:**  Reddit posts related to conspiracy theories. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gpreda/reddit-conspiracy-theory) 
23. **Worldnews on Reddit:** Posts from the Reddit "worldnews" subreddit. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rootuser/worldnews-on-reddit)
24. **Reddit on Israel-Palestine:** Reddit posts discussing the Israel-Palestine conflict. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/asaniczka/reddit-on-israel-palestine-daily-updated)
25. **Ukraine Subreddit Threads and Comments:** Reddit data related to Ukraine. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bwandowando/ukrainesubredditthreadsandcomments)
26. **Ukraine Subreddit Top Comments:** Top comments from Ukraine-related subreddits. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/akuppps/ukraine-subreddit-top-comments)
27. **Coronavirus (COVID-19) Tweets:** Tweets related to COVID-19. [Link](https://kaggle.com/datasets/smid80/coronavirus-covid19-tweets)
28. **COVID-19 NLP Text Classification:** COVID-19 related text data for classification. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/datatattle/covid-19-nlp-text-classification)
29. **Corona Various News COVID-19:** News articles about COVID-19. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/saadaljebrreen/corona-various-news-covid19)
30. **Coronavirus News Headline:**  News headlines related to the coronavirus. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sagunsh/coronavirus-news-headline)
31. **COVID-19 Vaccine News Reddit Discussions:** Reddit discussions about COVID-19 vaccines. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/xhlulu/covid19-vaccine-news-reddit-discussions)
32. **Coronavirus on Reddit:** Reddit data related to the coronavirus. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gpreda/coronavirus-on-reddit)
33. **Ukrainian Conflict Top Comments:** Top comments from subreddits discussing the Ukrainian conflict.  [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/akuppps/ukrainianconflict-top-comments)
34. **All Tweets (2015-2021):**  Tweets from a public figure (2015-2021). [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ayushggarg/all-trumps-twitter-insults-20152021)
35. **Ukraine-Russian Crisis Twitter Dataset:** Twitter data related to the Ukraine-Russia crisis. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bwandowando/ukraine-russian-crisis-twitter-dataset-1-2-m-rows)
36. **US Politicians Twitter Dataset:**  Tweets from US politicians. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mrmorj/us-politicians-twitter-dataset)
37. **Twitter Climate Change Sentiment Dataset:** Tweets about climate change with sentiment labels. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/edqian/twitter-climate-change-sentiment-dataset)
38. **The Reddit Climate Change Dataset:** Reddit posts related to climate change. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/pavellexyr/the-reddit-climate-change-dataset) 
39. **Climate Change Tweets:**  Tweets about climate change. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/die9origephit/climate-change-tweets)
40. **Twitter Financial News:**  Financial news from Twitter. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sulphatet/twitter-financial-news) 
41. **BBC News Archive:** Archive of BBC news articles. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hgultekin/bbcnewsarchive)
42. **Twitter Pfizer Anti-COVID:**  Tweets about Pfizer's anti-COVID vaccine. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gallo33henrique/twitter-pfizer-anticovid)
43. **Pfizer Vaccine on Reddit:** Reddit posts about the Pfizer vaccine. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gpreda/pfizer-vaccine-on-reddit) 
44. **Reddit Russia-Ukraine Conflict Dataset:** Reddit data related to the Russia-Ukraine conflict. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/tariqsays/reddit-russiaukraine-conflict-dataset)
45. **Russia-Ukraine Conflict:**  Dataset covering the Russia-Ukraine conflict.  [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hskhawaja/russia-ukraine-conflict)
46. **Public Opinion on Republicans (Daily Updated):** Reddit data reflecting public opinion on Republicans. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/asaniczka/public-opinion-on-republicans-daily-updated)
47. **Ukraine War:** Dataset related to the war in Ukraine. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sinatavakoli/ukraine-war)
48. **Israel-Hamas Conflict News Dataset:**  News articles about the Israel-Hamas conflict. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/emirslspr/israel-hamas-conflict-news-dataset)
49. **Palestine Tweet Data (2022):**  Tweets about Palestine from January to August 2022. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/madjiderroukrma/palestine-tweet-data-2022-januaryaugustar-en)
50. **Russia vs Ukraine Tweets Dataset (Daily Updated):**  Tweets related to the Russia-Ukraine conflict, updated daily. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/towhidultonmoy/russia-vs-ukraine-tweets-datasetdaily-updated)
51. **Russia-Ukraine Conflict:** Another dataset covering the Russia-Ukraine conflict. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aneeshtickoo/russia-ukraine-conflict)
52. **Fake News Detection:**  Code for fake news detection. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/code/adityarahul/fakenewsdetection)
53. **USA Presidential Debate 2020:**  Transcripts from the 2020 US presidential debates. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gpreda/usa-presidential-debate-2020)
54. **42-Minute Rant:** Transcript of a 42-minute speech by a public figure. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vyombhatia/donald-trumps-42-minute-rant)
55. **USA 2020 Elections Tweets:** Tweets related to the 2020 US elections. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jaidalmotra/usa-2020-elections-tweets)
56. **2024 US Presidential Elections Top News:** Top news articles about the 2024 US presidential elections. [Link](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/diegoexe/2024-us-presidential-elections-top-news)
